About Mawar Indah

Mawar Indah binti Abdullah is a historical collections librarian at Tengku Mardhiah Library, Terengganu. Get to know more about her and the services she provide.

Background

Mawar Indah is a professional librarian that specializes in historical collections and research support. With 7 years experience in library management, she is currently serving at Tengku Mardhiah Library, dedicating her skills in preserving and organizing materials of historical value. Mawar assists researchers, students, and the public in navigating historical archives, rare documents, and specialized databases.

Before she build her career in information management world as a historical collections librarian, Mawar holds a Bachelor of Information Science (Honors) Library Management from Universiti Teknologi Mara, where she developed a strong foundation in information management, cataloguing, and user services. During her studies, she developed a particular interest in historical resources and archival practices, which led her to pursue further specialization in this area.

After successfully graduating, Mawar started her career from the bottom as a junior librarian at a public library in her hometown, where she was assigned to supported daily opertions including circulation, shelving, cataloguing, and user services. She rotated through various library departments to gain foundational experience.

After 2 years, Mawar transitioned into a more research support focused role which is Reference and Information Servcies Librarian. She provided assistance to students and researchers in using academic databases and library resources and began working closely with faculty and postgraduate students in the humanities, particularly those using historical sources. Mawar also attended workshops and short courses in archival science and preservation.

In her 5th year as a librarian, Mawar has joined Tengku Mardhiah Library and formally apointed to a Special Collections librarian position, taking responsibility in organizing and maintaining rare books, historical documents, and archival materials. Not only that, she also collaborated with researchers on historical projects and curated small exhibitions or displays.

A year after that, she gets promoted into Historical Collections librarian, overseeing the management and preservation of historical resources.

Roles and Services Provided by Historical Collections Librarian

1) Collection Development & Preservation

Services provided includes acquisition services, preservation assessment, collection appraisal, environmental monitoring, and deaccession support.

2) Research & Reference Support

Services provided includes reference services, research consultations, document delivery, genealogy and local history assistance, and instructional support.

3) Outreach & Education

Services provided includes exhibition curation, classroom instruction, public programs and events, community engagement, and volunteer and internship supervision

4) Digital Initiatives

Digitization projects, digital curation, online access, metadata enhancement, and digital preservation.
